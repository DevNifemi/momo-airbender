KOKA INU is deflation token!

KOKA INU is going to have its own DEX and NFT market. Half of the fees from DEX/NFT market will be used for buyback from Uniswap and burn (it makes INU deflation).
The buyback and burn will be implemented after NFT market or DEX starts.
The buyback and burn will be implemented every month. This system makes INU deflation.
The supply is smaller every month. If the community keeps using DEX and NFT more often each month, the fees will be higher, which motivates the community token holder to use DEX and NFT market.
The price will be growing after the buyback. Remaining 50% will be used for marketing and development of new features to make INU great.
We rely on KOKA INU community and deflation of this Ethereum based token.
